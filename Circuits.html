The F1 Circuits

Monza

Name: Autodromo Nazionale Monza

Length: 5.793 km / 3.600 mi

Turns: 11 – most notably Variante del Rettifilo, Curva Grande, the Lesmo pair, Variante Ascari, and Curva Alboreto.

First Grand Prix: 1922

Lap Record: 1 min 21.046 s (2004, Rubens Barrichello)

Unique Feature: Long straights, three chicanes, limited run-off areas, etc.

More: home race to Andrea Kimi Antonelli.

Silverstone

Name: Silverstone Circuit

Length: 5.891 km / 3.661 mi

Turns: 18 – most notably Copse, Maggotts–Becketts, Chapel, and Stowe.

First Grand Prix: 1948

Lap Record: 1 min 27.097 s (2020, Max Verstappen)

Unique Feature: Notoriously tricky weather, high-speed straights, built atop of RAF Silverstone, a WWII airfield, etc.

More: home race to Lewis Hamilton, Lando Norris, George Russell, and Alex Albon.

Monaco

Name: Circuit de Monaco

Length: 3.337 km / 2.074 mi

Turns: 19 – most notably Sainte Devote, Casino Square, Fairmont Hairpin, and Rascasse.

First Grand Prix: 1929

Lap Record: 1 min 12.909 s (2021, Lewis Hamilton)

Unique Feature: Underground tunnel, no run‑off areas, big elevation changes.

More: home race to Charles Leclerc.
